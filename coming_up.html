Group Nautical Conference

A half day Seminar and Panel Discussions on the Topic “The Red Dot in the emerging Maritime Technologies” on January 26th, 2018, at the Keppel Club, Singapore. The objective is to discuss Challenges and Opportunities in emerging Maritime Technologies , their Impact and relevance in actual problem solving as well as also to highlight the Role and Position of Singapore in this arena.

We are bringing together some of the Brightest minds in the Industry from the Top Management Positions from Global Shipping Companies from the US, Europe and Far East ( Japan) and external subject matter experts on Block Chain, Big Data, Autonomous Technologies etc and Regulators e.g Class, Flag State, MPA etc.

  • Panel Discussions

    A half day Seminar and Panel Discussions on the Topic: “ The Red Dot in the emerging Maritime Technologies.

  • Multi Dimentional Speakers

    Some of the brightest minds as speakers from Top Management Positions are joining the conference.

  • Singapore's Role

    Discussions on Challenges and Opportunities in emerging Maritime Technologies and the Role and Position of Singapore in this arena.
