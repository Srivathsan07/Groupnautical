Why Us Group Nautical

Docuchain

Blockchain enables document management and exchange system for maritime users. It's safe, secure and transparent with full audit trails. Blockvchain chain gives functionality of pre-authentication of documents means faster turnaround.

EPI Center

EPiCentre is a real time Fleet Operations Performance Analytics solution with a pre-build BI Solution designed comprehesive monitoring. EPiCentre can be configured to runs as per your organisational objective.

MyDia

MyDiA is mobile app for contact and multi-user task management specifically developed for maritime users. It can also be integrated with DocuChain to give access to all documents from any device anywhere anytime.
